# ADR 0001 — Modular Monolith

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

Version 1 will use a modular monolith instead of microservices.

## Rationale

- Faster development
- Easier testing
- Lower deployment complexity
- Better suited for a small engineering team
- Easier onboarding

## Consequences

Positive:
- Simple architecture
- Faster iteration

Negative:
- Future scaling may require service extraction
