# FinSight AI – Product Vision

## Vision

Empower retail investors with institutional-grade investment intelligence by combining real-time financial data, machine learning, explainable AI, and natural language insights into a single platform.

## Mission

Make investing more informed, transparent, and accessible through AI-powered analytics and intuitive visualizations.

## Objectives

- Provide real-time portfolio tracking.
- Deliver explainable AI predictions.
- Analyze market sentiment using financial news.
- Help users understand investment risks.
- Offer intelligent recommendations without replacing human decision-making.

## Core Principles

- Transparency
- Explainability
- Accuracy
- Security
- Simplicity
- Performance
